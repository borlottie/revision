Hypotactic

Hypotactic is a scansion database that has more complete (and potentially more accurate) scansion for all of the Aeneid as well as other Latin works. You can use it to practice scansion for the Aeneid book 6 here. Other Aeneid books and Latin authors are also available. Note that it doesn't often mark caesuras and doesn't have notes on various exceptions. It also has the 6th foot of every line marked as a spondee which is incorrect. You'll want to use it with the "Show Macrons" checkbox in the sidebar toggled to off, and the "j/v" checkbox off as well if you like.

You can use the bookmarklet below (written by me) to view specific lines at a time in Hypotactic. Drag this link into your bookmarks bar, and then whenever you're on Hypotactic you can click it to bring up a dialogue letting you choose specific lines to view. Hypotactic View Lines
