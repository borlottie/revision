Hypotactic

Hypotactic is a scansion database that has more complete (and potentially more accurate) scansion for all of the Aeneid as well as other Latin works. You can use it to practice scansion for the Aeneid book 6 here. Note that it doesn't often mark caesuras and doesn't have notes on various exceptions. It also has the 6th foot of every line marked as a spondee which is incorrect. You'll want to use it with the "Show Macrons" checkbox in the sidebar toggled to off, and the "j/v" checkbox off as well if you like.

You can use the bookmarklet below (written by me) to view specific lines at a time in Hypotactic. Just drag this link into your bookmarks bar: Hypotactic View Lines
